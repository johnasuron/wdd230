About Lodwar

Lodwar is the capital city of Turkana County in Kenya. Known as the gateway to the north, it serves as a major economic hub for the region. With its strategic location and growing infrastructure, Lodwar offers numerous opportunities for businesses.

Business Opportunities

Agriculture

Lodwar's fertile lands provide excellent opportunities for farming and livestock rearing.

Retail & Trade

The bustling markets of Lodwar are ideal for retail businesses and trade activities.

Tourism

With proximity to Lake Turkana and other natural attractions, tourism is a promising sector.
